# VITRINE — Gift Atelier

Premium Telegram mini-app: craft and collect gifts with clear recipes and clear prices.

**Not a game loop.** No tap grind, no spins, no $ prize races, no mystery boxes.

## Why someone stays
- Daily materials (once per day)
- Craft gifts from visible recipes
- Gallery / collection flex
- Send a crafted gift to a friend

## Why someone spends
- Shop shows **exact** gift or material pack + Stars price
- “I want this piece” — not “maybe I win”

## Screens
1. **Home** — showcase + daily claim
2. **Atelier** — materials + recipes + craft
3. **Gallery** — owned gifts
4. **Shop** — clear-price Stars purchases (demo confirm for now)
5. **Send** — share a gift card in Telegram
