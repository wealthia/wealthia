Wealthia Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Wealthia is a Telegram Mini App game. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum.

What we collect

  • Telegram user ID, first name, and username (from Telegram when you play)
  • Game progress: coins, taps, buildings, tasks, leaderboard score
  • Referral links you use or share

How we use it

  • To save your game and sync progress across sessions
  • To run leaderboards, daily tasks, and tournaments
  • To give referral rewards when friends join via your link

Third-party services

  • Telegram — login and Mini App platform
  • Supabase — secure game data storage
  • AdsGram — optional rewarded ads (only if you choose to watch)

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not access your Telegram messages or contacts
  • We do not collect payment card details in the game

Data retention

Your game data is kept while you use the app. You may reset progress in the game settings.

Contact

Questions: contact us via our Telegram bot or channel.
