# MERGE ARENA

Telegram mini-app: **Merge · Upgrade · Dominate**

## Loop
1. Summon units (energy)
2. Drag same units together to merge / upgrade
3. Battle the wave
4. Win trophies + gems, climb waves
5. Buy exact power in Armory (Stars)

## Not included
- Tap grind
- Mystery loot boxes
- Fake $ prize races

## Monetization (exact)
- Full Energy — 25★
- +10 Energy — 15★
- Rare Summon — 40★
- Epic Summon — 90★
- Power Surge — 35★
- Gem Starter — 50★
